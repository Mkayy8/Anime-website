Hunter x Hunter(pronounced "hunter hunter") is a Japanese anime series created by Yoshihiro Togashi. The main character, Gon Freecss, is a young boy who's mission is to train himself as a hunter and find his father who left at birth. There are 7 story arcs/seasons currently however as of now there are no plans to make another.

One piece is a Japanese anime series created by Eiichiro Oda. The story follows the adventures of the main character, Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as the "One piece" in order to beome the next King of the Pirates.
